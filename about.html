Our Mission

At Parvarin Sanskar Foundation, we believe that no one should have to face hardship alone. Our mission is to connect those who want to help with those who need help, creating a network of support that strengthens communities.

We work tirelessly to ensure that donations reach the people who need them most, with transparency and efficiency at the core of everything we do.

Our Core Values

Our Journey

2022

Founded in a Small Community

Helping Hands began as a local initiative to help families affected by an economic downturn in our hometown.

2023

Expanded to Regional Operations

After three successful years, we expanded our operations to serve the entire region, helping thousands more.

2024

National Recognition

Our innovative donation matching system received national attention and was adopted by several other organizations.

2025

Pandemic Response

During the COVID-19 pandemic, we mobilized quickly to provide emergency assistance to vulnerable populations.

Present

Serving Communities Nationwide

Today, Helping Hands operates in all 50 states, with over 200 local chapters and partnerships with major corporations.
