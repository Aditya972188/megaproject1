Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make a donation?

You can make a donation through our website by visiting the "Donate" section in the navigation menu. We accept monetary donations as well as donations of clothes and food. Each donation type has its own page with specific instructions.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Parvarin Sanskar Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and all monetary donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. For donations of goods, you may be able to deduct the fair market value. We provide receipts for all donations.

Where do my donations go?

Your donations directly support our programs helping individuals and families in need. Financial donations fund our food distribution programs, shelter services, and community outreach. Donated goods are distributed to those we serve or sold in our thrift stores to fund our programs.

How can I volunteer with Parvarin Sanskar Foundation?

We welcome volunteers! You can sign up through our volunteer portal on the website, or contact our volunteer coordinator at volunteer@helpinghands.org. We have opportunities ranging from one-time events to ongoing commitments in various areas of our organization.

Can I specify where my donation should be used?

Yes, when making a monetary donation, you can designate it for a specific program or area of need. If you don't specify, your donation will be used where it's needed most. For goods donations, we distribute items based on current needs across our programs.
