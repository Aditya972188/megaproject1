Accepted Clothing Items

We accept clean, gently used clothing in good condition

Winter Coats

All sizes, especially children's and plus sizes

Professional Attire

For job interviews and employment

Children's Clothing

All sizes, especially infants and toddlers

Shoes

Gently used, pairs kept together

Blankets

Clean and in good condition

New Undergarments

New socks and underwear only (packaged)

Preparation Guidelines

  • Clean all items before donating
  • Check for stains, tears, or excessive wear
  • Fold items neatly or hang them
  • Pair shoes together with rubber bands
  • Pack similar items together in bags or boxes
