Most Needed Food Items

Canned Proteins

Tuna, chicken, beans, and peanut butter provide essential protein.

Whole Grains

Brown rice, whole wheat pasta, and oatmeal are always needed.

Canned Vegetables

Low-sodium vegetables help provide balanced nutrition.

Cooking Essentials

Cooking oil, flour, sugar, and spices help families prepare meals.

Food Donation Guidelines

  • Non-perishable items only (unless coordinated)
  • Unopened and in original packaging
  • Not expired or damaged
  • No homemade items (except through approved programs)
  • Glass containers accepted if properly packaged

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about food donations

We accept most non-perishable food items that are unopened and within their expiration date. This includes canned goods, dry goods, pasta, rice, cereal, and more. We also accept fresh produce when coordinated in advance. We cannot accept homemade items, opened packages, or expired food for safety reasons.

We can accept perishable food like fresh produce, dairy, and frozen items with advance notice and proper coordination. Please call our food bank at (123) 456-7892 to arrange perishable food donations. We cannot accept perishable items without prior arrangement.

Yes! We welcome food drives from schools, businesses, churches, and community groups. We can provide collection bins, posters, and guidance on most-needed items. Contact our food drive coordinator at fooddrives@helpinghands.org or call (123) 456-7893 to get started.

For safety reasons, we cannot accept any food that is past its expiration date. The only exception is if the item has a "best by" date (rather than "use by" or "expires on") and is still within 6 months of that date for non-perishable items.

We have several drop-off locations throughout the city. Our main food bank is open for donations Monday-Friday from 9am-4pm at 123 Charity St. We also have donation bins at all local Grocery Plus stores. For large donations, we recommend calling ahead to ensure we can accommodate your donation.
